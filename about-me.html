about me

My name is Mie, my pronounce are she/her, I'm 26 years old (born 8th of May, 1996) and I live in Copenhagen, Denmark.

I started out wanting to make a website because of watching my boyfriend work. Maybe I should elaborate a bit on that?
My boyfriend works as a software-engineer and being the "curious George" as I am, I sometimes wanted to see what he actually did while working. A little by little it started to interest me and I asked him if he could help me learn how to code and luckily, he said yes. After that I was able to see just how fun and versitale coding could be and I only wanted to learn more.

I have always had an interest in digital design and what makes a website, a picture, a logo and such things pleasant to look at and I wanted to see if there was an education that possibly combined all these things. And after a bit of searching I stumbled across an education called "Digital Media" and after reading a lot about what it could offer me, I decided to apply to the Digital Media-education in Roskilde.

What I really like about the education is that you learn a little about everything - coding, animation, graphic design etc, so it's perfect for someone like me who likes a bit of everything and not just the coding.
