gaming

Gaming has always been a big passion of mine.
When I was little, it was the opportunity of living in a completely different world or fantasy land that intrigued me. One of the very first games I ever played (and actually remember) was Pac-Man. I must've been around 4 or 5 when my dad would place me on his lap and play it with me and I immediately associated "playing games" with something fun and safe.

Not long after that, me and my sister got our very own PlayStation 1 so we could play Spyro the Dragon. I could sit there for hours and have the most fun in the world and especially after that game I was hooked.
When I got older, the fun that came along with playing wasn't the only thing I appreciated. I started to noticed the importance of the art-work, the voice acting, the storyline and just all the little details that makes a game a great one.

As of today, the consoles that I own (together with my boyfriend) are a PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, Nintendo DS and a Nintendo Switch. And of course, a computer for gaming. My personal favorite consoles to play on must be a tie between the PlayStation 4 and the Nintendo Switch. The PlayStation is perfect for more action-packed and long games where the Switch is more suitable for cozy little role-playing/arcade-like games, such as Pok√©mon, that you can just lay in the couch and relax with.

My favorite games? I'm a sucker for story-based games with a beautiful and touching story and a bit of action - so games such as The Last of Us (Part 1 and 2), the God of War games, the Uncharted games, the Spider-Man games, The Witcher III, the Assassins Creed games and Red Dead Redemption II are some of my absolute favorite games.
